Why Easy Flow Movers?

We are a moving company serving the Greater New Orleans area. Chances are you have already moved a few times in your life and just the thought of another move is stressful. You shouldn’t have to worry about the details of getting your things out of your old home and into the new. This transition should be a positive experience and we promise to do everything in our power to make the move easy on you. The main way we help our customers is by offering flexible moving services suited for all needs. When you contact us for a free moving estimate, an experienced moving agent will talk in detail about your exact needs and come up with a plan tailored for you. We can help with packing, organizing car transport or boat shipping, dismantling your furniture, and much more. You get all the professional moving services you need without having to pay for the ones you don’t need.

Local and Long Distance Moving Services

We provide the same level of care and attention to detail with every single move. With local moves, it means planning the move around your schedule but while taking into consideration factors like traffic, vehicle access, parking, and coordinating with building managers to use elevators and staircases. With long distance moves, we must consider numerous other factors to ensure that your belongings reach their destination as quickly as possible while keeping everything safe and affordable. With hundreds of moves completed, our team of moving professionals know how to coordinate to make sure your move flows as smoothly as possible!

Get Your Free Moving Estimate

Want to know how much your move will cost? Our moving rates are very competitive and priced fairly. Local moves are based on an hourly rate depending on how many movers you need. Long distance moves are priced by weight and mileage. We will gladly provide you with a free moving estimate based on your exact needs, including any specialty services you may need such as packing or vehicle shipping. Just fill out our contact form and include any relevant details to your moving needs for a fast, free, and accurate estimate!
